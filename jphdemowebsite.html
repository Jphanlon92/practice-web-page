Jack's Chincoteague Vacation Guide

Welcome! Are you planning a trip to Chincoteague Island on the eastern shore? If so, use this guide to help plan the best vacation.

Chincoteague Island is the ultimate family adventure location nestled on on the bottom of the eastern shore. Here you can see wild ponies in their natural habitat, relax on beautiful beaches, and even catch a NASA rocket launch from nearby Wallops Island. With fresh seafood, charming shops, and endless outdoor fun, Chincoteague is where nature and adventure come together for unforgettable memories.

Lodging

Chincoteague Island offers a range of lodging options perfect for every type of traveler. Families and outdoor lovers can enjoy camping at Jellystone ParkTM, which features cabins, tents, and plenty of kid-friendly amenities. For those seeking comfort and convenience, waterfront hotels like the Fairfield Inn & Suites and the Hampton Inn & Suites provide beautiful views and modern perks right near the island’s main attractions. Vacation rentals through Seaside Vacations & Sales and Coastal Shore Rentals offer more space and privacy, making them ideal for groups or longer stays. No matter your style or budget, you’ll find a welcoming place to stay on Chincoteague Island.

Dining

From fresh seafood to sweet treats, Chincoteague has it all. Breakfast and coffee enthusiasts can kickstart their day at Main Street Shop and Coffeehouse with a quality brew and a refreshing acai bowl. When lunchtime hits, refuel at Famous Pizza & Sub Shop, widely hailed for serving up the best subs on the island. You’re on island time, so when happy hour rolls around, slow down and head to Ropewalk. Sip a cocktail, soak in the vibe, and enjoy live music drifting through the salt air. When dinner calls, it’s time for an island staple,crabs! Steamers Restaurant & Sports Bar dishes out a wide variety of seafood and non-seafood entrées in a laid-back, family-friendly setting. Prefer your crab with a side of elegance? Head over to Pearl Waterside Dining & Deck Bar, where you’ll savor fresh seafood while gazing out at the stunning Assateague Channel. And of course, no culinary tour of Chincoteague is complete without dessert. The island’s most delicious rivalry? Creamery versus Mister Whippy. One’s famous for its rich, homemade ice cream; the other’s a soft-serve legend. You’ll just have to try both, strictly for research purposes, of course. With such a delightful variety of dining experiences, Chincoteague Island serves up something for every palate and then some.

Nature Activities

Assateague Island National Park is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Enjoy a peaceful bike ride or walk along the Wildlife Loop in the National Wildlife Refuge. On the loop you can spot wild ponies, waterfowl, and a variety of birds. Spend a day relaxing on the pristine beaches or try your hand at crabbing along the shore. Hike the Lighthouse Trail through maritime forest to reach the historic Assateague Lighthouse for incredible views and wildlife sightings. Whether you’re biking, hiking, birdwatching, beachbumming, or crabbing, the islands offer endless ways to connect with nature. Make sure you pack enough H2O for your fun adventures!

Places to explore on Assateague Island National Seashore

  1. Chincoteague National Wildlife Refuge: Wildlife Loop Walk or bike the loop and spot wild ponies, deer, and dozens of bird species. Enjoy wildlife photography, fishing, and educational programs.
  2. Assateague Island National Seashore Swim, sunbathe, or collect shells on a pristine beach. Watch for wild ponies along the dunes!
  3. Crabbing on Assateague Try your luck at crabbing! It's fun for all ages, with gear available locally.
  4. Assateague Lighthouse Trail Take a short scenic hike to this historic lighthouse and climb for panoramic views.

Island Activities

Chincoteague Island offers a variety of fun activities beyond its natural beauty. Visit the Chincoteague Pony Centre to meet the famous ponies up close and learn about their history. Explore Historic Main Street for unique shopping, local dining, and special events. Browse handmade goods at the Farmers and Artisans Market. Enjoy family-friendly attractions like Surfside Golf, Funland Amusements, and Maui Jack’s Waterpark. Catch a classic film at the historic Island Theater, where “Misty of Chincoteague” premiered. Lasty visit the Wallops Visitor Center to experience NASA’s space adventures. With so many island activities, there’s something memorable for everyone in your group

The image shows the Island Theater, a vintage Art Deco building located in the heart of Chincoteague’s Main Street district. Its neon marquee glows with the words “Island Theater,” casting vibrant red and yellow light onto the sidewalk below. The facade is classic white stone with geometric accents, and the entrance is framed by two glass doors under the bright lights. Above the marquee, a vertical sign towers into the dusk sky, and you can see hints of the town’s charm in the surrounding buildings. The Island Theater is not just a movie venue but a local landmark, famous for its role in the premiere of “Misty of Chincoteague” and as a gathering spot for community events.

Things to do in the town of Chincoteague

  • Chincoteague Pony Centre Meet real Chincoteague ponies, attend a show, or try a pony ride.
  • Historic Main Street & Mall Stroll local boutiques, galleries, and cafes. Special events year-round.
  • Farmers and Artisans Market Shop for produce, crafts, and art with a local touch.
  • The Island Theater See a movie or relive the story of Misty of Chincoteague.
  • Surfside Golf Play a round of seaside-themed mini-golf.
  • Funland Amusements Classic arcade and rides for family fun.
  • Maui Jack’s Waterpark Cool off with slides, a lazy river, and splash zones.
  • NASA Wallops Flight Facility Visit the NASA Visitor Center for interactive exhibits, rocket launch information, and space science activities. Check the schedule to catch a real rocket launch!
